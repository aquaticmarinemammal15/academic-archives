# Neuron

Specialized cell that communicates via [[Electrical signaling]] (within) and [[Chemical signaling]] (between). Major types: [[Sensory neuron]], [[Interneuron]], [[Motor neuron]]. Parts: [[Cell body]], [[Dendrite]], [[Axon]].
