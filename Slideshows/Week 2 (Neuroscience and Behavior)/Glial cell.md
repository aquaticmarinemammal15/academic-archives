# Glial cell

Non-neuronal support cells providing structure, metabolism, [[Myelination]], cleanup, and immune defense. Types: [[Astrocyte]], [[Microglia]], [[Oligodendrocyte]], [[Schwann cell]], [[Ependymal cell]].
