# Sex vs Gender

Sex ≠ Gender. [[Sex]] refers to biological aspects (e.g., [[Chromosome]]s, [[Gonad]]s, [[Anatomy]], [[Physiology]]). [[Gender]] refers to psychological, social, and cultural aspects (e.g., [[Masculinity]], [[Femininity]]).
